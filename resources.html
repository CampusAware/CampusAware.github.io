Posters

Here we provide downloadable posters in small (8.5x11) and large (11x17) format. These posters are intended to be placed on university campuses with the aim of bringing awareness surrounding harmful messaging that affect Jews on campus. We have posters designed for Equity, Diversity, and Inclusivity offices who wish to bring awareness with a soft message. We have other posters designed for Jewish student or advocacy groups who may wish to use a make a stronger statement.

Different universities have different poster policies. Some provide open bulletin boards that community members can place posters on. Others require pre-approval of posters before they can be put up. Users of our posters are personally responsible to ensure they are compliant with postering policies on their university campus. Campus Aware is not responsible for any such violations.

Please place the name of your group placing up the posters on the designated section of the poster together with an email address for your group that you can be contacted at.
